About Me

I am a Filipino currently based in Hamburg. I graduated with Bachelor of Science in Nursing in the year 2012 from West Visayas State University. I did a year volunteer work in different hospitals in the Philippines before working in a Psyciatric Facility in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for 2 years. While in Saudi Arabia, I voluntered to be a multi media coordinator in an underground Church service where I designed Typography content honed my skills in Photoshop and desingning. I also served as a translator to English language for Chruch attendees who do not speak the Filipino language. Currently, I am working as a staff nurse in a surgical ward in one of the biggest hospitals in Hamburg since 2018.


I possess a positive, energetic approach to working collaboratively with others, solving problems and provide evidence-based, and holistic approach client-centered care and services. Within my nursing education and clinical experiences, I have the privilage of working in a wide variety of health-care settings. Now, my interest is on the IT sector and I am expanding my role as web developer. Since learning about Redi School of Digital Integration, I took IT courses that opened variety of oppurtunities for me to lear new things and grow as an individual.


My commitment to life long learning is without question and I believe that I would be a fit candidate for any role in the IT sector. I look forward to discuss with you my suitability for the position further. Feel free to download my CV below and contact me.
